蛮石营造-万科数字植物园

阿制2025-01-062025-01-06

**蛮石营造**：用自然 . 科技 . 艺术来大胆创造了一个VANKE未来社区的数字植物园，用科技更好的保护和认知生物的多样性，用智慧培育更多濒危植物及可食植物，用交互体验更深入的感受来自未来科技和自然生态的融合，并助力万科人居环境的智慧化升级，这也是万科对未来社区生活方式的实践和探索。

MOOOONS: Use nature, technology, and art to boldly create a digital botanical garden for the future community of VANKE, use technology to better protect and recognize biological diversity, use intelligence to cultivate more endangered and edible plants, use the interactive experience to deeply feel the integration of future technology and natural ecology, and assist the intelligent upgrade of Vanke’s living environment, which is also Vanke’s practice and exploration of future community lifestyle.

▽万科数字植物园 VANKE Digital Botanical Garden

整个万科数字植物园项目由数字植物舱及虚拟植物园两部分组成，并包含了植物营造，AI智慧+植物，交互体验以及数字艺术等跨界内容。万科未来社区的空间体验已由传统式迭代为智慧式，数字化，沉浸式的体验。

The entire project of Vanke Digital Botanical Garden consists of a digital plant cabin and a virtual botanical garden, which includes cross-border content such as plant creation, AI + plants, interactive experience, and digital art. The space experience of the future community of Vanke has been iterated from traditional to an intelligent, digital, and immersive one.

NO1：数 字 植 物 舱 DIGITAL PLANT CABIN

数字植物舱分为三部分：无土栽培层 、智慧植培层、以及一个植物舱控制系统。上层为无土栽培区，下层为有土植培区，上下由一个大屏系统来连接和控制。我们用这样的方式构建了一个微小的智慧化生态环境，未来我们能在舱体内去培育和养护更多的植物种类，甚至是濒危植物。

The digital plant cabin consists of three parts: soilless cultivation layer, intelligent cultivation layer, and a plant cabin control system. The upper layer is the soilless cultivation area, the lower layer is the soil cultivation area, and the upper and lower layers are connected and controlled by a large screen system. In this way, we have built a tiny intelligent ecological environment. In the future, we can cultivate and maintain more plant species in the cabin, even endangered plants.

无土栽培层：Soilless cultivation layer:

无土栽培是一种新型的室内的植物栽培方式，又名营养液培养法。这种营养液能代替自然土壤向植物体提供水分、养分、温度等生长因子，使植物能够正常生长并完成其整个生命周期。

我们用这种方式可种植不同种类的可食植物和蔬菜瓜果，在VANKE未来社区中，居民可以参与到可食植物的种植 . 养护 . 采摘等各个环节中去，真正体验到现代化农业的乐趣，是万科对于未来生活方式的一种探索和实践。

Soilless cultivation is a new indoor plant cultivation method, also known as the nutrient solution cultivation method. The nutrient solution can replace the natural soil to provide growth factors such as water, nutrients, and temperature to the plants so that they can grow normally and complete the entire life cycle.

In this way, we can grow different kinds of edible plants, vegetables, and fruits. In the future community of VANKE, residents can participate in the cultivation, maintenance, and picking of edible plants, and truly experience the fun of modern agriculture, which is Vanke’s exploration and practice of future lifestyle.

智慧植培层：Intelligent cultivation layer:

利用IOT技术：数据化，在线化，算法化，智能化，将一个最传统行业管理通过AI+方式重塑。数据化由各类传感器实现–包括各类环境数据营养液及土壤的温度，湿度，土壤肥力，光照强度，空气温度，空气湿度等；算法化在云服务器上实现；智能化则通过各类智能硬件实现无人化操作。同时，将整个过程再通过可视化方式展现给用户。

Use IoT technology: through dataization, onlinization, algorithmization, and intelligentization, reshape the management of one of the most traditional industries via the AI+ method. Dataization is realized by various sensors–including various environmental data, nutrient solution, soil temperature, humidity, soil fertility, light intensity, air temperature, air humidity, etc. Algorithmization is realized on cloud servers. Intelligentization is realized via various Intelligent hardware to achieve unmanned operation. Meanwhile, the whole process is presented to the users in a visual manner.

植物舱控制系统：Plant cabin control system:

植物舱控制系统将传感器采集到的数据传递到远程云服务器，云服务器根据特定的植物模型，利用算法实现植物生长的无人化管理，我们可在植物舱前面的交互大屏上看到植物环境的实时可视化数据。

The plant cabin control system transmits the data collected by the sensors to the remote cloud server. The cloud server uses algorithms to achieve unmanned management of plant growth according to specific plant models. We can see the real-time and visual data of the plant environment on the large interactive screen in front of the plant cabin.

舱体控制系统设置为两种模式：全自动模式+手动控制模式

目前项目使用的是全自动模式，在全自动模式下植物舱依据植物生长模型的支撑，算法的控制，实现生长周期光温湿全自动管理；手动模式可支持手动的个性化养护，但需要对植物有一定基础知识的用户，目前我们可通过手机远程操作辅助调节各项参数。

The cabin control system has two modes: fully automatic mode + manual control modeThe current project uses the fully automatic mode. In which, the plant cabin is based on the support of the plant growth model and controlled by the algorithm to realize the automatic management of light, temperature, and humidity in the growth cycle. The manual mode supports manually personalized maintenance, but it requires the users to have a certain basic knowledge of plants. Currently, we can assist in adjusting various parameters via the remote operation on the mobile phone.

植物舱远程控制系统：Plant cabin remote control system:

我们在上海通过手机端app可远程查看到植物舱内的各项数据，对各项异常数据和警报做出处理，以保障植物正常的生长环境，在未来，全国各地的项目不论数量和距离，所有的数据都将传输到上海，由我们远程辅助操作，这将很大程度上减少运营人员和运营费用。

In Shanghai, we can remotely check various data in the plant cabin on the mobile app, and deal with abnormal data and alarms to ensure the normal growth environment of plants. In the future, regardless of the number and distance of projects across the country, all data will be transmitted to Shanghai and operated by us remotely, which will greatly reduce operating personnel and expenses.

▽通过手机端远程操控 Remote control via mobile phone

NO2：虚 拟 植 物 园 VIRTUAL BOTANICAL GARDEN

虚拟植物园是我们将自然植物和虚拟数字植物两种截然不同的体验结合在一起打造的沉浸式场景，步入虚拟植物园就像来到奇妙的垂直森林，在虚实之间穿梭，每个参与者都是携带数字种子基因密码的使者，由一颗种子开始一段未来旅行。

The virtual botanical garden is an immersive scenario created by combining two distinct experiences of natural plants and virtual digital plants. Stepping into the virtual botanical garden is like coming to a wonderful vertical forest. Shutting between the virtual and the real, each participant is an envoy carrying a digital seed genetic code, starting a future journey with a seed.The journey of a seed.

▽概念设计 The conceptual design

▽生态垂直森林 Ecological vertical forest


一颗种子的旅行 The journey of a seed

通过手机扫描二维码，每个人会得到一颗数字化虚拟种子，我们将种子带到垂直森林里种下，感受一颗植物的发芽，生长，开花的过程。越来越多的虚拟植物在这里生长，它们的数字基因被储存起来，森林就是一个生物多样性的博物馆，后期植物园的数据库也可以一直更新。

By scanning the QR code with the mobile phone, everyone will get a digital virtual seed. We will plant the seed in the vertical forest, so as to feel the process of germination, growth, and flowering of a plant. More and more virtual plants will grow here, their digital genes will be stored, making the forest a museum of biodiversity. In the future, the database of the botanical garden can also be updated all the time.

▽扫描二维码，完成种子的虚拟而奇妙的一生 Scan the QR code to complete the virtual and wonderful life of the seed

▽沉浸式体验 Immersive experience

我们用数字虚拟植物的概念创作了一套数字艺术作品，抽象又富有想象力的虚拟植物与垂直森林融合在一起形成强烈又丰富的视觉冲击，是对于未来自然与艺术的一种表达。此类虚拟植物主题的数字作品也会以NFT的方式进行呈现，个人也可以购买和收藏极具未来的植物数字作品了。

We have created a set of digital artworks with the concept of digital virtual plants. The abstract and imaginative virtual plants and the vertical forest are integrated to form a strong and rich visual impact, which is an expression of future nature and art. Such digital works on the theme of virtual plants will also be presented in the form of NFT, and individuals can also buy and collect digital plant works with a bright future.

▽数字化与植物形成强烈的视觉效果 Digitalization creates a strong visual effect with plants

在整个植物场景的营造上分为两种风格，植物园区域选择较为自然野性的风格，植物配置上也更多偏向热带感受，大量使用了蕨类，苔藓及热带植物。在中心咖啡区则更为简洁干净，大面积的苔藓的使用给人更多禅意感受。

There are two styles in the creation of the entire plant scene. The botanical garden area chooses a more natural and wild style, and the plant configuration is more inclined to the tropical feeling, which has used a lot of ferns, moss, and tropical plants. In the central coffee area, it is more concise and cleaner, and the use of a large area of moss gives people more sense of meditation.

▽植物园区大量使用热带植物，延续自然野性的风格 The botanical garden uses a large number of tropical plants to continue the nature of the wild style


▽中心咖啡厅简洁干净，大面积的苔藓给人禅意感受 The central cafe is simple and clean, with a large area of moss giving people a Feeling of Zen

本次数字植物园项目的最大的挑战还是来自多专业的跨界合作，虽然工作室一直践行打破专业边界的原则，但过程中多专业配合还是遇到很多困难，思维逻辑和工作方法都存在差异，但可能正是因为不同才能爆发能量吧，项目因为是从设计到施工的全流程工作，所以施工阶段对现场的品控也是挑战之一，当然万科甲方的专业和高效也是确保品质的关键，对于这类跨界项目需要各方敢于创新和挑战才能最终完美呈现。

The biggest challenge of this digital botanical garden project is the cross-border cooperation among multiple professions. Although the studio has always practiced the principle of breaking the professional boundary, there are still many difficulties in the process of cooperation since there are differences in thinking logic and working methods. However, it may be because of the difference that the energy can explode. Because the project is a whole process work from design to construction, the on-site quality control at the construction stage is also one of the challenges. Of course, the professionalism and efficiency of Vanke are also the key factors to guaranteeing quality. For such a cross-border project, all parties need to dare to make innovations and meet challenges before it can be presented in a perfect manner.
